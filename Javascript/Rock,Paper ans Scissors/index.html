Rock-Paper-Scissors

How to Play?

In this game computer will be one player
The user will choose one of the three choices(Rock,paper,scissors)
Computer will keep track of the choice and try to defeat its opponent
Rules:Each player will choose one of rock, paper or scissors
Both the players will not be aware of the others has choosen.
The winner is decided by set of rules
Rock blunts scissors
Paper covers rock
Scissor cut paper
If both player choose same thing,then this game will withdraw
